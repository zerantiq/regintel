# Warning Thresholds

Use these labels for concrete milestones only.

## Labels

### Critical Deadline

Use when:
- the date is overdue, or
- the milestone is within 30 days, or
- a mandatory reporting/disclosure obligation is within 14 days

Urgency: `Critical`

### High Priority

Use when:
- enforcement or effective date is within 31 to 60 days, or
- a material readiness gap is visible and the milestone is within 90 days

Urgency: `High`

### Action Needed Soon

Use when:
- the milestone is within 61 to 120 days, or
- the rule is already effective and the repo shows likely missing controls

Urgency: `High`

### Upcoming Change

Use when:
- the milestone is within 121 to 270 days, or
- the item is adopted and planning should begin this cycle

Urgency: `Medium`

### Monitor

Use when:
- the milestone is more than 270 days away, or
- the item is still proposed with no near-term obligation

Urgency: `Low`

## Notes

- Escalate one level if the repo scan shows likely missing controls tied to the milestone.
- De-escalate if applicability is weak or company context is missing.
- Do not use deadline labels for vague “watch this space” developments.
